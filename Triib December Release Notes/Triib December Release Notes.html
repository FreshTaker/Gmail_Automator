What’s new: 

  • The ability to change the app color is here!  
  • To make this change head over to the admin site Design Settings -> General Design Settings. On the right hand side you will see this screen. 

  • Make sure the box is checked for the mobile app and select “Set as Theme Color”. Next time your members log into the app they will see the updated color change! 

What’s improved: 

  • Both iOS and Android apps have an updated Agenda Tab.  
  • Coaches are now able to see the classes they are assigned to coach.
  • Trainers and members can now see upcoming appointments.
  • Trainers are able to see the client name for each appointment.
  • Members can see the classes they have RSVP’d too, along with any upcoming appointments scheduled and who it is with. 

What’s fixed:
